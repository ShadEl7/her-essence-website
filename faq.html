Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about shopping with H.E.R.

Orders & Shipping

How long does shipping take? +
Standard delivery within Namibia takes 3-5 business days. Express delivery is available for 1-2 business days. International shipping takes 7-14 business days depending on your location.
Do you offer free shipping? +
Yes! We offer free standard shipping on all orders over N$500 within Namibia. Use code FREESHIP500 at checkout.
Can I track my order? +
Absolutely! Once your order is shipped, you'll receive a tracking number via email. You can also track your order by logging into your account or contacting our customer service.
What if my order is damaged during shipping? +
We're sorry to hear that! Please contact us within 48 hours of receiving your order with photos of the damage. We'll arrange a replacement or full refund immediately.

Returns & Exchanges

What is your return policy? +
We offer a 30-day return policy. Items must be unworn, unwashed, and in original condition with tags attached. Return shipping is free for defective items or our error.
How do I return an item? +
Visit our Returns page to start a return. Print the prepaid shipping label, package your item securely, and drop it off at any courier service point.
Can I exchange for a different size? +
Yes! We offer free exchanges for size or color within 30 days. Simply start a return and place a new order, or contact us to arrange a direct exchange.
When will I receive my refund? +
Refunds are processed within 3-5 business days of receiving your returned item. The refund will appear on your original payment method within 5-10 business days.

Sizing & Fit

How do I find my size? +
Check our detailed Size Guide which includes measurements for all our clothing items. When in doubt, we recommend sizing up for a comfortable fit.
Do your clothes run true to size? +
Our clothing generally runs true to size, but fit can vary by style. Each product page includes specific fit notes and model measurements to help you choose the right size.
What if I'm between sizes? +
If you're between sizes, we generally recommend sizing up for comfort. You can also contact our styling team for personalized advice based on the specific item you're interested in.

Payment & Account

What payment methods do you accept? +
We accept all major credit cards (Visa, Mastercard, American Express), PayPal, EFT, and mobile payments. All transactions are secure and encrypted.
Do I need an account to place an order? +
No, you can checkout as a guest. However, creating an account allows you to track orders, save addresses, view order history, and receive exclusive member benefits.
Is my payment information secure? +
Absolutely! We use industry-standard SSL encryption to protect your payment information. We never store your full credit card details on our servers.
Can I modify or cancel my order? +
You can modify or cancel your order within 1 hour of placing it by contacting us immediately. After that, your order may have already been processed for shipping.

Product Care

How should I care for my H.E.R. clothing? +
Care instructions are included with each item and on the product page. Generally, we recommend washing in cold water, air drying when possible, and following the care label instructions.
Are your fabrics high quality? +
Yes! We source premium fabrics and materials for all our pieces. Each item is designed to be durable, comfortable, and maintain its quality wash after wash.
What if my item shrinks or fades? +
If you follow our care instructions and experience unexpected shrinking or fading, please contact us. We stand behind our quality and will work to resolve any issues.

Customer Service

How can I contact customer service? +
You can reach us via email at info@heressencerestored.com, phone at +264 81 234 5678, or WhatsApp. Visit our Contact page for all options.
What are your customer service hours? +
Our customer service team is available Monday-Friday 9AM-6PM WAT, and Saturday 10AM-4PM WAT. WhatsApp and email support is available 24/7 with responses within 24 hours.
Do you have a physical store? +
Currently, H.E.R. operates as an online boutique to bring you the best selection and prices. We're exploring physical locations for the future - stay tuned!

Still Have Questions?

Our friendly customer service team is here to help! We're committed to making your H.E.R. experience exceptional.
