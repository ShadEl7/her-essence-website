Her Essence Restored – Privacy & Data Policy

Effective Date: November 1, 2025

At Her Essence Restored, we are committed to protecting your personal data and ensuring your privacy. This policy outlines how we collect, use, store, and protect your information.

2. How We Use Your Data

  • Fulfill and ship orders

  • SSL encryption site-wide
  • Secure third-party payment processors

4. Third-Party Services

  • Delivery companies (e.g., NamPost, DHL, Courier Guy)

  • For any requests, email: privacy@heressencerestored.com

6. Marketing Communications

  • We only send marketing communications with your explicit consent.
  • You may unsubscribe anytime via the link in our emails.

7. Cookies

  • You can disable cookies through your browser settings, but this may affect site functionality.

8. Updates to This Policy

  • Updates will be posted here with the date at the top.
