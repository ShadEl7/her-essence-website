Discover how to style your favorite pieces from Her Essence Restored. Get inspired by our curated looks and tips from our stylists!

Vintage Vibes

Pair a classic vintage dress with a wide-brimmed hat for timeless elegance. Add pearl earrings and a delicate clutch for a complete retro look.

Modern Chic

Combine a structured blazer with trendy sneakers for a fresh, modern look. Layer with minimalist jewelry and a crossbody bag for effortless style.

African Prints

Make a statement with bold African prints and handcrafted accessories. Mix patterns and textures for a vibrant, unique ensemble.

Casual Comfort

Opt for relaxed jeans and a cozy sweater for everyday comfort. Finish the look with slip-on shoes and a tote bag for errands or coffee dates.

Evening Glam

Shine at night with an elegant evening gown and statement jewelry. Choose metallic heels and a clutch for a touch of glamour.
