Style Guide & Inspiration

Discover how to style your favorite pieces from Her Essence Restored. Get inspired by our curated looks and expert styling tips!

Style Categories

  • Vintage Vibes
  • Modern Chic
  • African Prints
  • Casual Comfort
  • Evening Glam

Vintage Vibes

Pair a classic vintage dress with a wide-brimmed hat for timeless elegance. Add pearl earrings and a delicate clutch for a complete retro look that never goes out of style.

Modern Chic

Combine a structured blazer with trendy sneakers for a fresh, modern look. Layer with minimalist jewelry and a crossbody bag for effortless style that transitions from day to night.

African Prints

Make a statement with bold African prints and handcrafted accessories. Mix patterns and textures for a vibrant, unique ensemble that celebrates cultural heritage and personal style.

Casual Comfort

Opt for relaxed jeans and a cozy sweater for everyday comfort. Finish the look with slip-on shoes and a tote bag for errands, coffee dates, or weekend adventures.

Evening Glam

Shine at night with an elegant evening gown and statement jewelry. Choose metallic heels and a clutch for a touch of glamour that commands attention at any special event.
