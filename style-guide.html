Style & Size Guide

Discover how to style your favorite pieces from Her Essence Restored. Get inspired by our curated looks and expert styling tips!

Dresses

Discover our stunning collection of dresses perfect for any occasion. From casual day dresses to elegant evening wear, find the perfect dress that makes you feel confident and beautiful.

Style Tips:

  • Choose A-line for universally flattering silhouette
  • Midi length works for most body types
  • Add a belt to define your waist
  • Layer with jackets for versatile styling

Wedding Gowns

Make your special day unforgettable with our exquisite wedding gown collection. Each gown is carefully selected to make you feel like royalty on your wedding day.

Style Tips:

  • Ball gown for classic princess look
  • Mermaid cut to accentuate curves
  • Consider train length for venue
  • Choose neckline that flatters your body

ActiveWear

Stay active in style with our premium activewear collection. Designed for comfort and performance, our pieces transition seamlessly from workout to everyday wear.

Style Tips:

  • High-waisted leggings for support and coverage
  • Moisture-wicking fabrics for comfort
  • Layer sports bras for added support
  • Mix athleisure pieces for casual chic

Modern

Embrace contemporary fashion with our modern collection. Clean lines, innovative designs, and versatile pieces that reflect today's fashion-forward woman.

Style Tips:

  • Minimalist silhouettes for timeless appeal
  • Neutral colors for versatile mixing
  • Quality fabrics for professional settings
  • Structured pieces for polished looks
