H.E.R Return & Refund Policy

At H.E.R (Her Essence Restored), we want you to feel beautiful, confident, and completely satisfied with your purchase. If something isn’t quite right, we’re here to help.

Returns

We accept returns within 14 days of receiving your order.

To be eligible for a return, items must be:

  • Unworn and unwashed
  • In their original condition with all tags attached
  • Free of stains, scents, or damage
  • Accompanied by proof of purchase (receipt or order number)

Non-returnable items:

  • Sale or discounted items
  • Gift cards
  • Custom or made-to-measure sundresses
  • Accessories (earrings, scarves, etc., for hygiene reasons)

Refunds

Once we receive and inspect your returned item, we’ll notify you of the approval or rejection of your refund.
If approved, you can choose between:

  • Full refund to your original payment method
  • Exchange for a different size or style (based on availability)

Refunds are processed within 7–10 business days after we receive the return.

Return Process

  1. Email us at info@heressencerestored.com with your order number and reason for return.
  2. We’ll send you return instructions and a return form (if required).
  3. Ship your item back to:
    H.E.R Returns
    [Your Return Address Here]

*Note: Customers are responsible for return shipping unless the item arrived damaged or incorrect.*

Need Help?

If you received a damaged or incorrect item, please contact us within 48 hours of delivery.
Send a photo and description to info@heressencerestored.com so we can make it right.
